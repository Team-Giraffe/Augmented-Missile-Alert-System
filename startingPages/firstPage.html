EMERGENCY ALERT SYSTEM

TEST ALERT

FOLLOW THE STEPS BELLOW:

1. Obtain verbal command of a test run.

2. Ensure that all emergency departments acknowledge the drill.

3. Ensure that there are no emergencies occurring at the moment

REAL ALERT

FOLLOW THE STEPS BELLOW:

1. Obtain verbal command and written confirmation of emergency.

2. Ensure that all emergency departments are aware of the current emergency.
